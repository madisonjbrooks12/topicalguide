Section 8.2 Bulk Sales . The Purchaser hereby waives compliance by the Sellers and their Affiliates with the requirements and provisions of any “bulk-transfer” or similar laws of any jurisdiction that may otherwise be applicable with respect to the sale, conveyance, assignment or transfer of any or all of the Acquired Assets to the Purchaser.
