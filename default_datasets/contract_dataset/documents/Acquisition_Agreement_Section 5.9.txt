Section 5.9 Break-Up Fee and Expense Reimbursement . Notwithstanding anything in this Agreement to the contrary, and if approved by the Bankruptcy Court pursuant to the Stalking Horse Order, the Sellers agree to pay the Purchaser the Break-Up Fee and the Expense Reimbursement Amount in accordance with Section 7.2(b) of this Agreement and the Stalking Horse Order.
