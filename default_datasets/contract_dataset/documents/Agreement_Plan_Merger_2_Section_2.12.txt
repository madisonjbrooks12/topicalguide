Section 2.12     Escrow Fund. In accordance with the Escrow Agreement, Parent shall deposit or cause to be deposited with the Escrow Agent the Indemnification Escrow Amount (such amount, including any interest or other amounts earned thereon and less any disbursements therefrom in accordance with the Escrow Agreement, the “ Escrow Fund ”), to be held for the purpose of securing the indemnification obligations of the Stockholders, Optionholders and Warrantholders set forth in this Agreement and the obligations pursuant to Section 2.17(d) and Section 6.03 .
